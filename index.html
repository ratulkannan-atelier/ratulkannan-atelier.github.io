4SIGHT

Shopify Inventory Reorder Predictor. From zero to production on AWS.

Shopify shows merchants their current stock. It doesn't tell them when it runs out. 4Sight connects to the Shopify GraphQL Admin API via real OAuth 2.0, computes a 30-day rolling sales velocity per product with a 20% safety buffer, and ranks products by stockout urgency, so merchants reorder before the gap, not after.

Research

JET CALIBRATION PIPELINE

Developed a novel high-throughput jet calibration pipeline for the ATLAS detector at CERN using the Forward Folding technique, modelling detector response and resolution to align measured data with simulation. Migrated iterative loops to NumPy vectorizations, resolving major computational bottlenecks. Achieved a 97% reduction in data discrepancy through statistical modelling, ensuring reproducible physics results.
